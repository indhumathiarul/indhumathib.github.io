Who am I?

Hey, I'm Indhu. I'm a backend engineer with a deep love for building things — and an even deeper love for understanding how they work.

I started writing technical guides because I wanted the kind of resource I wished existed when I was learning. Not just surface-level tutorials, but deep, practical, example-driven guides that take you from zero to confident.

I've written 26+ comprehensive guides covering everything from gRPC and design patterns to AWS services and distributed systems — and I'm just getting started.

I believe tech should be accessible, practical, and interesting. That's what this corner of the internet is about.
